Sports Betting Insights & Tips

Understanding Probability

Odds represent probability. Lower odds mean a higher chance of happening.

Common Terms Explained

  • Favorite
  • Underdog
  • Push
  • Handle
  • Bankroll

Beginner Tips

  • Learn the terms
  • Compare odds
  • Understand uncertainty
  • Focus on learning

Example Explanation

If a team is -150, they are favored. If a team is +150, they are the underdog.
